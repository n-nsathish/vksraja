Welcome to VKS Raja

   We RAJA SIDHAA MARUNDAGAM was commenced in the year 1961. We are one of the leading manufacturers of Siddha and Ayurveda medicines in South India. We are serving to the public for the past 60 Years. A quality guarantee is the main purpose of our business. We distribute our products all over Tamilnadu, India. We are certified with GMP, TRADEMARK and ISO certifications. Our product range consists of Lehyam, Rasayanam, Chooranam, Mathirai, Capsule, Vennai, Gugglu, Syrup, Thailam, Theeneer, Mezhughu, Kudineer Chooranam, Parpam, Chendooram. Our units are split into manufacturing, finishing, quality control, clinical trials, warehouse and packaging.

Our Specialities

சித்தா

Siddha

ஆயுர்வேதம்

Ayurveda

Our Product Types

லேகியம்

Lehyam

Lehyam is semi solid in consistency. It is prepared from mentioned drugs or herbs with the addition of jaggery with prescribed drug juices

தைலம்

Thailam

Thailam is an oil based medicine. It consist of powerful herbs. It used for many external diseases

சூரணம்

Chooranam

Chooranam is a traditional formulation used by Siddha practitioners. The texture of chooranam is in powder form. We consume it with water or honey

ரசாயணம்

Rasayanam

Rasayanam is generally pleasant and has jam like consistency. It develops immunity and helps in restoring physical and mental health

டானிக்

Syrup

Syrup is the liquid medicine, that is consumed by the Individuals in different measures as a solution to various diseases
