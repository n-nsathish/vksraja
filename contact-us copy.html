Welcome to VKS Raja

   We RAJA SIDHAA MARUNDAGAM was commenced in the year 1961. We are one of the leading manufacturers of Siddha and Ayurveda medicines in South India. We are serving to the public for the past 60 Years. A quality guarantee is the main purpose of our business. We distribute our products all over Tamilnadu, India. We are certified with GMP, TRADEMARK and ISO certifications. Our product range consists of Lehyam, Rasayanam, Chooranam, Mathirai, Capsule, Vennai, Gugglu, Syrup, Thailam, Theeneer, Mezhughu, Kudineer Chooranam, Parpam, Chendooram. Our units are split into manufacturing, finishing, quality control, clinical trials, warehouse and packaging.
